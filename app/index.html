About our crew

Our President is Zaphod Beeblebrox, also the President of the Galaxy. He was voted “Worst dressed Sentient Being in the Known Universe” seven consecutive times. His brain is fuelled by lemons, but has good hunches.

Ford Prefect is from a planet in the vicinity of Betelgeuse, and a semi half cousin of Zaphod Beeblebrox. They share three of the same mothers, and both favor the Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster. Marvin is a robot with an exceptionally large brain, and built in Genuine People Personality. He is always happy and eager to help. Or is he? Arthur Dent is an Englishman, who happened to be friends with Ford Prefect, who happened to choose to take Arthur to the Galactic ride. Tricia McMillan, or Trillian, which sound more spacey.She is also a human, who happened to meet Arthur and Zaphod at the same fancy dress party on Earth, and happened to choose to check out Zaphod‘s spaceship.

Humma Kavula is a political rival of Zaphod Beeblebrox, who just happens to be in the picture, but it might turn out as a good campain catch for Zaphod.

Our Features

We’ll Translate

We supply You with a cute little, yellow fishy thingy. Just put it in your ear, do not worry it only tickles a bit. Wait for it, waaaaait for it, and here You go. You can now understand all creatures in the Universe.

Galactic Guide

We will also supply You with a small, thin, flexible digital guide, which is the standard repository for all knowledge and wisdom. The most important wisdom is also inscribed on its cover, in large, friendly letters.

Robot Companion

To make Your journey even more unforgettable, we will introduce You to Marvin. He has a brain of the size of a planet, and built in GPP, as we previously mentioned. Have fun! Or not.

Vogon Poetry

This feature is not for the faint of heart, but if You think You are brave enough to attend, we will not deny You the opportunity to a Vogon poetry reading. Enjoy! Or not.
